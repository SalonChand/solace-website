Our Story

About Solace at Sundown

Where nature meets luxury and every sunset tells a story.

Our Beginning

Born From a Love of Sunsets

Solace at Sundown was born from a simple yet profound belief — that every person deserves a place where the pace of life slows down, where the air is clean, and where the evening sky paints peace across the heart.

Nestled at the edge of Shivapuri National Park in Kathmandu, our retreat was created to offer more than just a stay. It's an invitation to reconnect — with nature, with stillness, and with the people who matter most.

Every detail, from the placement of our sunset-facing camps to the curated campfire evenings, is designed to help you step away from the noise and into something meaningful.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by a deep respect for nature, a commitment to comfort, and a passion for creating lasting memories.

Nature First

We operate with deep respect for the environment, ensuring our presence uplifts rather than disrupts the natural ecosystem.

Genuine Hospitality

Every guest is treated like family. We listen, we care, and we go above and beyond to make your stay unforgettable.

Quiet Luxury

Luxury isn't loud — it's in the details. From premium linens to candlelit dinners, we elevate every moment with quiet sophistication.

Community

We support local communities, source ingredients locally, and employ talented individuals from the surrounding villages.

Sustainability

From solar energy to waste reduction, sustainability is woven into every decision we make at Solace at Sundown.

Mindful Living

We create spaces for reflection, digital detox, and conscious living — encouraging every guest to slow down and breathe.
